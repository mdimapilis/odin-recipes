Buttermilk Pancakes

Description

This simple recipe for buttermilk pancakes is easy to follow and for home cooks! If you like light and airy pancakes with an abundance of flavor then this recipe is for you. Pancakes are one of the most delicious breakfast foods and this recipe will help you master making pancakes.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Leaveners
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients: Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Mix the wet ingredients: Beat the buttermilk, eggs, and melted butter together in a separate bowl. Do not combine the wet and dry ingredients until right before you cook the pancakes.
  3. Make the pancakes: Combine the wet and dry ingredients. Scoop the batter onto a hot, oiled griddle and cook until the pancake is bubbly on the top. Flip with a spatula and cook until both sides are brown.
